### I'm new to Jot

Choose your path:

**Path A: Beginner (No SQL)**
- **Time**: 15 minutes | **Goal**: Get comfortable with basic note management
- 1. Start: [Getting Started: Beginner's Guide](getting-started-basics.md) - Simple intro
- 2. Then: Create notebooks and manage notes naturally
- 3. When ready: Graduate to [Getting Started for Power Users](getting-started-power-users.md)
- **Result**: You can manage, search, and organize your notes ‚úÖ

**Path B: Power User (With SQL)**
- **Time**: 15 minutes | **Goal**: Get productive with SQL queries
- 1. Start: [Getting Started for Power Users](getting-started-power-users.md) - Complete 15-minute onboarding
- 2. Then: [Import Workflow Guide](import-workflow-guide.md) - Understand how to organize your notes
- 3. Next: [SQL Quick Reference](sql-quick-reference.md) - Learn Level 1 (Basic Queries)
- **Result**: You'll have imported your notes and run your first SQL query ‚úÖ

### I'm migrating from another tool

**Time**: 30 minutes | **Goal**: Move your existing notes to Jot

1. Pick your source: [Import Workflow Guide](import-workflow-guide.md)
   - Coming from Obsidian? See "Obsidian Vault Import"
   - Coming from Bear? See "Bear Notes Migration"
   - Coming from generic markdown? See "Generic Markdown Folder"
2. Then: [Getting Started for Power Users](getting-started-power-users.md) - Try your first query
3. Reference: [SQL Quick Reference](sql-quick-reference.md) - Query your migrated notes

**Result**: Your notes are imported and queryable ‚úÖ

### I want to automate my workflows

**Time**: 1-2 hours | **Goal**: Set up automated reporting and backups

1. Start: [Getting Started for Power Users](getting-started-power-users.md) - Understand basics
2. Learn SQL: [SQL Quick Reference](sql-quick-reference.md) - Complete Levels 1-4
3. Automate: [Automation Recipes](automation-recipes.md) - Ready-to-use scripts
4. Reference: [JSON-SQL Guide](json-sql-guide.md) - Data transformation patterns

**Result**: You have automated daily/weekly reports ‚úÖ

### I'm having problems

**Time**: 5-15 minutes | **Goal**: Resolve your specific issue

1. Go to: [Getting Started Troubleshooting Guide](getting-started-troubleshooting.md)
2. Find your error or symptom
3. Follow the step-by-step solution

**Result**: Your problem is solved ‚úÖ

### I want to manage multiple notebooks

**Time**: 20 minutes | **Goal**: Organize notes across projects

1. Read: [Notebook Discovery](notebook-discovery.md) - Multi-notebook patterns
2. Create notebooks: See "Getting Started for Power Users" - Part 1
3. Reference: [SQL Quick Reference](sql-quick-reference.md) - Switch between notebooks

**Result**: You have organized notebooks with clear discovery ‚úÖ
